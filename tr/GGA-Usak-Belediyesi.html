Uşak Municipality Building is activated by integrating the urban public spine into the building. Dokuz Sele river is seen as a recreation spine of the New Uşak urban center that is being transformed. The Municipality building is seen as the center of this transformation by being integrated to the river. The most public usages such as cafeteria and kinder-garden are located on the ground level whereas more private office usages are located on the first floor. The top floor serves mostly for the least public usages and administrative usages.
