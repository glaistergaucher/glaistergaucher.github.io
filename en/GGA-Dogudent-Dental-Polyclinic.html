Doğudent Dental Polyclinic

program:

polyclinic, 150m2


location:

Istanbul, 2014


status:

built

Doğudent Dental Polyclinic consists of three storeys and aims to change the traditional understanding of clinics and to create comfort in health sector. Creating this space, the project offers a cosy environment with the right selection of both materials and colors. With the practical and clean solutions for clinic box units the optimum space usage is provided. This leaves the maximum possible area for the public areas such as waiting and reception spaces.
