Fulya Dental House

program:

conversion, clinic, home office


location:

Fulya, Istanbul, 2014


status:

built

The interior is designed as a transformation from an existing apartment into a dental clinic. The renovation now offers a fully functional clinic, comprised of an entry hall / reception desk and waiting area, as well as an operation room. The space is evenly divided and its volume maximised by the simplicity of the custom-built furniture. The sense of hygiene expected in such an operation room overflows to the rest of the design, from the reflective surfaces to the use of varying tones of grey.
